Experience the Divine

Immerse yourself in the spiritual heart of India with our meticulously curated pilgrimage to Varanasi. This is not just a tour, but a journey inward. Day-wise Itinerary

1

Arrival & Evening Ganga Aarti Day 1

Pickup from Varanasi Airport/Railway Station. Check-in at your premium hotel. In the evening, proceed to Dashashwamedh Ghat to witness the world-famous Ganga Aarti from a reserved boat position.

Main Temple Darshan & Rudrabhishek Day 2

Early morning holy dip in the Ganges. Proceed for VIP Darshan at the main Shiva Temple. Participate in a special Rudrabhishek Puja conducted by Vedic pandits in your name. Later, visit Annapurna Temple and Kal Bhairav Temple.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code for the temples? expand_more
Modest attire is required. Shoulders and knees must be covered. For men, dhoti-kurta is preferred but not mandatory; trousers and shirts are acceptable. Women are advised to wear sarees or salwar kameez. Leather items might be restricted in inner sanctums.
Can I customize the dates? expand_more
Yes, we offer private customized tours for families or groups. Please contact our support team using the "Contact Host" button to request specific dates outside our scheduled departures.
Is airport transfer included? expand_more
Yes, complimentary pick-up and drop-off from Varanasi Airport (VNS) or Varanasi Junction Railway Station are included in the package.
